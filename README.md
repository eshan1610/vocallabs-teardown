## Company Chosen: Vocallabs.ai

Vocallabs builds AI voice agents that automate business calls with human-like fluency — sales, support, and booking. India-first, full-stack, founder-led.

I picked Vocallabs over Subspace because the harder product to tear down is the more interesting one. The GTM gaps, the moat narrative, and the structural UX problems are more consequential here — and more fixable.

---

## The 5 Feedbacks

| # | Feedback | Pillar | Priority |
|---|----------|--------|----------|
| 1 | The Self-Serve Blackhole: 'Book a Demo' is leaking your fastest-paying segment | UX | P0 |
| 2 | 'India-First' is a claim, not a moat — nobody can see the proof | Competitor Analysis | P1 |
| 3 | Voice Analytics is bullet #5. It should be the lead product. | Features / Services | P2 |
| 4 | There's no ICP. 'Businesses' is not a target segment. | GTM & ICPs | P1 |
| 5 | The Shopify x D2C wedge is a Rs. 100 Cr problem nobody has claimed yet | Potential Collaborations | P0 |

Each feedback is structured as: **Observed → Problem → Ship instead.**
